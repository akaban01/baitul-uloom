Why It Matters

You're not just funding a school. You're funding the start of one.

Right now, every dollar Bayt ul Uloom receives goes toward the most foundational work: registering and maintaining our nonprofit status, covering basic operating costs, and building the reserve that will let us take the next steps — securing curriculum, hiring our first staff, and eventually finding a site.

There is no overhead of an existing campus to maintain. Your support goes toward building something new — efficiently, and from day one.

About tax-deductibility

Bayt ul Uloom is organized as a nonprofit corporation and is in the process of finalizing its 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status with the IRS. Once approved, donation receipts will reflect this status, and the determination will apply retroactively to the date of our incorporation, as is standard for new nonprofits. We're happy to answer any questions about our current status — just reach out.
