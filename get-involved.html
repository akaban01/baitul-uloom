Founding Committees

Find where your skills fit.

We're organizing our early volunteers into small working committees. Pick the one that matches your background and interest — you don't need any special qualifications to start, just willingness to help.

Curriculum & Education

For educators, curriculum developers, and Islamic studies teachers. Help shape what students will learn and how — from academic standards to our Qur'an and Arabic programs.

Fundraising & Development

For anyone with experience in fundraising, grant writing, or nonprofit development — or anyone willing to learn. Help us build the founding fund.

Outreach & Communications

For writers, designers, social media folks, and connectors. Help us tell our story and grow our community of supporters.

Facilities & Site Search

For anyone with knowledge of real estate, construction, zoning, or local property — help us scout and evaluate future locations.

Operations & Governance

For those with legal, accounting, HR, or nonprofit administration backgrounds. Help us set up the systems and structure Bayt ul Uloom needs to operate responsibly and grow sustainably.
