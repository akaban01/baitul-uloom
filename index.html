A house of knowledge, built by the community, for the community.

Bayt ul Uloom is a newly established non-profit working to build a full-time Islamic school rooted in Qur'an, Sunnah, and academic excellence. We're at the very beginning — and we're inviting you to help lay the foundation.

Where We Are Today

We're building something from the ground up — and that means right now.

Bayt ul Uloom does not yet have a school building, a campus, or enrolled students. What we do have is a founding team, a clear mission, and a community that believes this is needed. Today, our work is about laying the groundwork: forming the organization, building relationships, raising the funds, and planning the curriculum that will shape the school to come.

In progress

Forming the organization

Establishing Bayt ul Uloom as a registered nonprofit, building our founding board, and putting the governance structure in place that will guide the school for years to come.

Just starting

Building our community

Connecting with families, educators, and community members who share this vision — and who will help shape what Bayt ul Uloom becomes.

Planned

Raising the founding fund

Every school needs a place to start. We're working toward the funding needed to secure curriculum, staff, and eventually a site — and every contribution counts toward that goal.

What We Stand For

Three pillars, one purpose.

Everything we plan to build rests on the same foundation — faith, knowledge, and character, woven together rather than taught apart.

Qur'an & Deen

A strong, joyful grounding in the Qur'an, the Sunnah, and the foundations of our faith — taught in a way that students carry with them for life.

Academic Excellence

A rigorous, well-rounded education that meets state academic standards and prepares students to thrive in any path they choose.

Character & Community

An environment where adab, sincerity, and service to others are practiced daily — and where families feel like part of one extended community.

Our Journey

How we get from an idea to an open door.

We believe in being transparent about where we are. Here's the road ahead — and where Bayt ul Uloom stands on it right now.

  • Phase 1 · Underway

    Founding & community building

    Registering Bayt ul Uloom as a nonprofit organization, forming our founding board, and connecting with families and supporters who share our vision.

  • Phase 2 · Next

    Fundraising & planning

    Growing our founding fund, developing our curriculum framework and educational philosophy, and building partnerships with local scholars and educators.

  • Phase 3 · Underway

    Site & staffing

    We've leased our first home at 12425 River Ridge Blvd #2, Burnsville, MN 55337. Now we're preparing the space, hiring founding teachers and staff, and getting everything ready to welcome our first students.

  • Phase 4 · Future

    Doors open, inshā'Allāh

    Bayt ul Uloom welcomes its first class — and the real work of building a generation of confident, knowledgeable, and grounded young Muslims begins.
